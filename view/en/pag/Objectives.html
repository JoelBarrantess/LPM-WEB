Free access to technology

We are dedicated to providing free access to technology and digital education to all people, regardless of financial resources.

Educational programs and workshops

We offer a variety of educational programs and workshops aimed at fostering digital literacy and helping people develop their technological skills.

Additional services and resources

We provide additional services and resources to the community: Books, magazines, free Wi-Fi network services and other technological resources.

Commitment to digital inclusion

We are based on using technology and digital education as essential tools to improve people's quality of life.
